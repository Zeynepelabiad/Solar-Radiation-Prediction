About

Renewable energy is a hot and important topic nowadays. With the rise in oil prices and the move towards decreasing and eventually halting fossil fuel production, it is important for countries to know how they will replace energy production from fossil fuels. Solar energy is one of the renewable energy sources that has been growing rapidly and has the potential to replace some of the energy production from fossil fuels. Understanding how fast solar energy will grow can give us insights into how rapidly we can reduce our dependence on fossil fuels.

This web app predicts solar radiation using a solar radiation database from Kaggle. The web app will provide data science functions such as visualizations, some statistics, and predictive models for people who are interested in the Renewable Energy field. The dataset was given by Nasa for the NASA hackathon. The datasets are meteorological data from the HI-SEAS (Hawai’i Space Exploration Analog and Simulation) weather station from four months (September through December 2016).
